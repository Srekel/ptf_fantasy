Pocket Task Force: Fantasy Edition
Mission: The Recipe

Briefing

You're approached by an alchemist. She tells you that there's an alchemy contest coming up and that there's a wizard that lives in a tower who has been working on a potion. The alchemist really wants the recipe for the potion so she can win the contest.

Objectives

Mission goal: Get the recipe to the alchemist.

Bonus objective A: No suspicious harm done - this will start a crime investigation, which is undesirable. [Reward: 10]

Bonus objective B: Leave no trace of the purpose of your mission. For example, just copy the recipe instead of stealing it. [Reward: 10]

Bonus objective C: Sabotage the wizard's recipe to set back his research. [Reward: 10]

Bonus objective D: Kill the wizard. [Reward: 10]

Bonus objective E: Kidnap the wizard. [Reward: 20]
