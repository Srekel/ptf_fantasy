All players are BOTH the gamemaster AND agents in a traditional fantasy setting. The characters work for "The Shadow Guild", who strives for power through subterfuge and secret missions.
=================================================================

         | Strength | Agility   | Wisdom       | Charisma
------------------------------------------------------------
Human    |          |           |              |
Elf      | Scrawny  | Energetic | Shrewd       | Pretentious
Dwarf    | Burly    | Slow      | Stubborn     | Intolerant
Halfling | Weak     | Limber    | Resolute     | Affable
Orc      | Swole    | Athletic  | Simpleminded | Unpleasant
Goblin   | Fragile  | Quick     | Impulsive    | Nasty

                       Attack      |  Block
----------------------------------------------
Sword and shield:      Agility     |  Strength
Daggers:               Agility     |  Agility
Battle-axe:            Strength    |     -
Bow:                   Agility     |     -
Crossbow:              Wisdom      |  Strength

* Pick race + two weapons.
* Add/Modify an attribute. (Examples: Superswole, Tolerant, Patient)

=================================================================

Combat:
* D20 for initiative.
* Two actions per turn.

=================================================================

EVERYTHING is resolved like so:
* Discuss possible outcomes.
* Distribute their probabilities 1-20.
* Roll D20.
