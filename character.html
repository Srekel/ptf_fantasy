Weapons Action Damage Rule
  • Rolls on actions performed by character are rolled with a number of D6 (six-sided dice).
  • The result of a die roll counts as following: 1: Critical fail. 2-3: Fail. 4-5: Success. 6: Critical success.
  • 1D6 means: One six sided die.
  • AD6 means: Look at character's Agility. Roll that many D6.
  • SD6, WD6, CD6 means: Same as AD6 but look at Strength, Wisdom, Charisma.
  • AD6 * Strength for an action means: Roll AD6. Count the number of successes. Multiply that number with the character's Strength.
